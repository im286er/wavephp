## 入口index.php内容

```php
    header('Content-Type:text/html;charset=utf-8');
    error_reporting(0);

    require dirname(__FILE__).'/../../wavephp/Wave.php';
    $config = dirname(__FILE__).'/protected/config/main.php';
    
    $wave = new Wave($config);
    $wave->run();
```
